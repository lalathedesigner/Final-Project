About

Welcome! My name is LaChristy Young. I am a student graphic designer. My concentration is on Web Development, Digital Art, and Advertising. I've come a long way to find myself & discover my talents. I want to promote BIG thinking, & POSITIVE dreaming in my personal & professional life. I see my art as a way of bringing ideas to life. That is why i decided to name my business LucidDreams.

LucidDreams is my start-up company. I provide freelance design services as well as promotional services. I work with everyday people to design posters & collages, screen-print designs, digital illustration, etc. I work with other small businesses to creat promotional materials for them. They range from logos & business cards, to flyers & brochures, etc.

Although I am still learning, I take pride in everything that I do as if it will be displayed on a global scale. I love having the balance of working with people and working with technology to make art. I think it is a very special industry & I am proud to be a part of it! Below are a few examples of my work.

BEFORE WE MEET

If you wish to schedule a consultation with me, I need you to have the following information in mind:

  • What kind of project you are interested in.
  • When you would like to have it completed.
  • Reliable contact information for scheduling.
  • Your general ideas for the project. (Pictures & sketches welcomed, but not mandatory)
  • A smile! (Art for me is a release from negativity, please prepare for excitement & positivity)

LOCATION

Currently, I do not have any personal office space. However, I am happy to accommodate my clients. Whether it's the comfort of your own home, or the public library, or even Starbucks. I will be sure to make all considerations when scheduling our consultation.
